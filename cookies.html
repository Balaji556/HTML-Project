Cookies Policy

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile phone) when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites function more efficiently and to provide information to website owners. Cookies allow websites to remember your preferences, enhance your user experience, and gather analytics to improve the site.

How We Use Cookies

On our music website, we use cookies to:

Enhance User Experience: Remember your language and music preferences to make your browsing experience more personalized. Analytics & Performance: Track visitor behavior through platforms like Google Analytics, helping us improve the content and features on our site. Streaming Functionality: Ensure smooth playback of music and video content on the site by remembering your media preferences. Advertising: Deliver relevant ads based on your interests and monitor the effectiveness of our marketing campaigns.

Types of Cookies We Use

1. Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly. Without these cookies, certain services or features of the site may not be available to you.

Example: Cookies that manage your login session.

2. Performance Cookies

These cookies collect anonymous information about how visitors use the website. They help us understand how visitors interact with the site, allowing us to improve performance and functionality.

Example: Google Analytics cookies that track user interactions.

3. Functional Cookies

These cookies allow the website to remember choices you’ve made, such as your language preference, to provide a more personalized experience.

Example: Cookies that remember your preferred language or region.

4. Advertising Cookies

These cookies are used to show you relevant advertisements on third-party websites, based on your browsing activity on our site. They also help us measure the effectiveness of our advertising campaigns.

Example: Cookies used by advertising platforms like Google Ads or Facebook Ads.

Managing Cookies

You can control and manage cookies in various ways:

Browser Settings: Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can choose to block or delete cookies, though this may affect your experience on our site.

Cookie Banner: When you first visit our site, you will be presented with a cookie banner where you can manage your cookie preferences. You can choose to accept or reject non-essential cookies.

Third-Party Cookies

Our website may also use third-party cookies for services like music streaming, social media integrations, and analytics. These cookies are set by external companies, and their privacy practices are governed by the respective third-party’s privacy policies.



For example:

Spotify Widgets: If you play embedded music from Spotify on our website, Spotify may place cookies on your device to manage its services. YouTube or Vimeo Videos: Embedded music videos may set cookies to track video views and user interactions.

Cookie Duration

Cookies may be stored on your device for varying lengths of time. Some cookies are "session cookies," meaning they are only stored while your browser is open and are deleted once you close your browser. Other cookies are "persistent cookies," meaning they remain stored on your device even after your browser is closed until they expire or are deleted manually.

We may update this Cookies Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal reasons. We recommend reviewing this policy regularly to stay informed about how we are using cookies.
